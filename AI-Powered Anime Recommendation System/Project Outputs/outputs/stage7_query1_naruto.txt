Here are three anime recommendations similar to *Naruto*, based on your interest in action, ninja themes, and epic character growth:

---

**1. *Tsubasa Chronicle***  
**Plot Summary**: Syaoran embarks on a cross-dimensional journey to recover feathers linked to his lost friend, Princess Sakura, while encountering allies like a exiled ninja (Kurogane) and a talking meat-bun creature (Mokona). The story blends magic, romance, and high-stakes adventures across fantastical worlds.  
**Why It Matches**: Like *Naruto*, it combines action-packed battles with a deep, serialized plot and a cast of diverse, memorable characters. The supernatural elements and themes of friendship/destiny resonate with *Naruto*'s storytelling style.

---

**2. *Ninin ga Shinobuden***  
**Plot Summary**: A schoolgirl, Kaede, gets entangled in the chaotic world of a ninja-in-training (Shinobu) and her eccentric mentor, Onsokumaru. The series mixes slapstick comedy, parody, and light ninja action as Kaede navigates this bizarre universe.  
**Why It Matches**: If you enjoy *Naruto*'s blend of humor and ninja culture, this lighthearted parody offers similar whimsical charm and over-the-top antics, though with a more comedic focus.

---

**3. *Rurouni Kenshin: Meiji Kenkaku Romantan***  
**Plot Summary**: After the Meiji Restoration, former assassin Kenshin Himura lives as a wandering swordsman seeking redemption. He confronts former comrades and rivals, grappling with his violent past while protecting others.  
**Why It Matches**: Like *Naruto*, it centers on a protagonist with a tragic backstory striving for peace. The historical samurai setting and intense battles mirror the epic stakes and character-driven drama of *Naruto*'s later arcs.
